PRIVACY POLICY

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains how Galactic Barrage handles your data across the cosmos.

Last Updated: January 2025
01

Introduction

Welcome to Galactic Barrage! This Privacy Policy describes how Ramram Dev ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you play our game. By playing Galactic Barrage, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

We believe in transparency. Galactic Barrage is designed to collect minimal data while delivering an epic gaming experience.

02

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our game:

  • Game Progress: Your high scores, achievements, and level progression are stored locally on your device.
  • Device Information: Basic device type and operating system version for optimization purposes.
  • Usage Analytics: Anonymous gameplay statistics to help us improve game balance and features.
  • Leaderboard Data: If you opt-in, your username and scores may be displayed on public leaderboards.
03

How We Use Your Data

The data we collect is used exclusively for:

  • Saving your game progress and preferences
  • Displaying your achievements on global leaderboards (with your consent)
  • Analyzing gameplay patterns to improve game mechanics
  • Fixing bugs and technical issues
  • Preventing cheating and maintaining fair play

We never sell your personal data to third parties.

04

Data Storage & Security

Your game data is primarily stored locally on your device. For leaderboard functionality, data is transmitted securely using industry-standard encryption protocols. We implement appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction of your data.

Local storage means you're in control. Clear your game data anytime through your device settings.

05

Third-Party Services

Galactic Barrage may integrate with the following third-party services:

  • Game Services: Google Play Games or Apple Game Center for achievements and leaderboards
  • Analytics: Anonymous usage tracking to improve gameplay experience
  • Advertising: Optional rewarded ads may be served through trusted ad networks

Each third-party service operates under its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review their policies.

06

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: Request a copy of your stored data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data from our servers
  • Opt-Out: Disable analytics and personalized advertising
  • Portability: Export your game progress data

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information below.

07

Children's Privacy

Galactic Barrage is suitable for players of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately, and we will take steps to delete such information.

08

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Significant changes will be notified through the game or other communication channels. Continued use of Galactic Barrage after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

09
