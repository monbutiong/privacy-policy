PRIVACY POLICY

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains how Hill Hop handles your data as you build, race, and conquer every hill.

Last Updated: June 2026
01

Introduction

Welcome to Hill Hop! This Privacy Policy describes how Ramram Dev ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you play our 2D car-building hill-racing game. By playing Hill Hop, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

We believe in transparency. Hill Hop is designed to collect minimal data while you focus on building unique cars and conquering every hill.

02

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve your hill-racing experience:

  • Vehicle Builds: Your custom car configurations, part combinations, and upgrade choices are stored locally on your device.
  • Race Progress: Hill completion times, finish-line records, and level progression are saved to track your achievements.
  • Device Information: Basic device type and operating system version for performance optimization across different terrain types.
  • Usage Analytics: Anonymous gameplay statistics to help us balance hill difficulty and car-building mechanics.
  • Leaderboard Data: If you opt-in, your username and hill-climb times may be displayed on public leaderboards.
03

How We Use Your Data

The data we collect is used exclusively for:

  • Saving your custom car builds and garage configurations
  • Tracking your hill-climbing progress and finish-line achievements
  • Displaying your best times on global leaderboards (with your consent)
  • Analyzing race patterns to fine-tune hill physics and vehicle balance
  • Fixing bugs, optimizing terrain generation, and improving frame rates
  • Preventing cheating and maintaining fair competition

We never sell your personal data to third parties.

04

Data Storage & Security

Your car builds, race records, and game progress are primarily stored locally on your device. For leaderboard functionality, data is transmitted securely using industry-standard encryption protocols. We implement appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction of your data.

Local storage means you're in the driver's seat. Clear your game data anytime through your device settings.

05

Third-Party Services

Hill Hop may integrate with the following third-party services:

  • Game Services: Google Play Games or Apple Game Center for achievements and leaderboards
  • Analytics: Anonymous usage tracking to improve hill physics and car-building balance
  • Advertising: Optional rewarded ads may be served through trusted ad networks

Each third-party service operates under its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review their policies.

06

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: Request a copy of your stored data, including car builds and race history
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data from our servers
  • Opt-Out: Disable analytics and personalized advertising
  • Portability: Export your garage configurations and race progress data

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information below.

07

Children's Privacy

Hill Hop is suitable for players of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately, and we will take steps to delete such information.

08

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Significant changes will be notified through the game or other communication channels. Continued use of Hill Hop after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

09

Contact Us

Have questions about this Privacy Policy? We're here to help you get back to racing.
