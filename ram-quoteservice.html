Privacy Policy

Ram ServiceQuote values your privacy. This application is designed with an Offline-First architecture, meaning your business information remains stored locally on your device.

Information We Store

• Business Information
• Customer Details
• Quotes & Invoices
• Inventory Records
• Bookings & Calendar Events

Cloud Backup

If you choose to use Google Drive Backup, your encrypted database is uploaded directly to your personal Google Drive account. We do not store your data on our own servers.

Permissions

The application may request access to:

✔ Notifications (Booking reminders)
✔ Calendar (Google Calendar Sync)
✔ Storage (Database Backup & PDF Export)

Security

Your information remains under your control. We never sell your personal or business information to third parties.
