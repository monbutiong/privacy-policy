Privacy Policy

Ram ServiceQuote values your privacy. This application is designed with an Offline-First architecture, meaning your business information remains stored locally on your device by default.

Information We Store

All of the following data is stored locally on your device:

  • Business Information
  • Customer Details
  • Quotes & Invoices
  • Inventory Records
  • Bookings & Calendar Events

Cloud Backup

If you choose to use Google Drive Backup, your encrypted database is uploaded directly to your personal Google Drive account. We do not store, transmit, or have access to your data on our own servers — the connection is between your device and Google alone.

Security

Your information remains under your control at all times. We never sell your personal or business information to third parties. Backups are encrypted, and all data operations are transparent and initiated by you.
